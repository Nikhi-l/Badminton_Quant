# Welcome to Racket Sports Analytics! 🏸🏓

AI-powered video analysis for badminton and table tennis.

## What can I do?

- **Analyze Instagram Reels** - Share a link and get detailed analytics
- **Track Shuttlecock/Ball** - See object trajectory throughout the rally
- **Measure Smash Speed** - Get speed measurements in km/h
- **Pose Estimation** - Analyze your body positions and form
- **Generate Heatmaps** - Visualize your court coverage
- **Identify Weaknesses** - Get personalized improvement tips

## How to use

1. Select your sport (Badminton or Table Tennis)
2. Paste an Instagram reel URL
3. Wait for analysis (1-2 minutes)
4. Review your results and download the report!

## Supported Video Sources

- Instagram Reels
- Local video files (coming soon)
- POV footage from Meta glasses (coming soon)
