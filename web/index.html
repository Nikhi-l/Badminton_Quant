    How it works

    01

    Upload

    Any length, any phone. We build a low-res proxy for analysis and keep your original for the final render.

    02

    Rally detection

    Gemini watches the proxy and timestamps every rally — serve to floor — then ranks them by length and intensity.

    03

    Virtual camera

    Runpod vision adds player pose and shuttle tracks; the crop follows the action and renders from your full-res frames.

    04

    Beat-synced stitch

    Longest rallies first, court audio kept, a 120 BPM bed underneath, packed into a vertical story-ready reel.
